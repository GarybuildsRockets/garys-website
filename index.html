About Me

I am a student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign transfering to the University of Illinois at Chicago to pursue a mechanical engineering degree. My love of engineering stems primarily from automobiles and small aircraft and now with my desired major I can pursue a career in motorsport.

At UIUC, I’ve expanded into new areas including two-stage rocketry, avionics, and engineering design projects. This portfolio is a place to highlight my work and document the skills I’m developing in CAD, electronics, and more. I am excited to keep exploring, learning, and work on some awesome projects.
